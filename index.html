How I got my start

Like several interesting, weird, 'raised-online' people in my age demographic, I built the foundation of my coding knowledge by customizing my pet's pages in the popular virtual petcare website, Neopets.

Years later, nearing my 30th birthday and following the unexpected separation from my employer of 8 years; a friend of mine, and Front End engineer, shared the experiences of a former team member in tech. They had gone through a Grand Circus bootcamp and had excelled! With my interest in both coding and linguistics, I decided to take the leap into learning coding.
